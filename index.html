How to use


1.

Press the Randomize! button.

2.

Copy the outputted text into a text editor, such as Notepad, Google Docs, etc.

3.

Try your best to write down their definitions without looking it up.

4.

Check their definitions once you have them down for each word. Try again from step 1 if you made a mistake.

5.

You're all set! You've now memorized the definitions of each word.
I have to manually update this site, the words
will not update to newer ones automatically.
